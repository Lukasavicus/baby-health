## User Story

Como cuidador, eu quero registrar vitaminas e remédios com dose e horário para manter o controle da medicação do bebê.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Form with fields: name, dose, unit, time, and frequency
- Preset list of common medications available
- Option to log immediately or set up recurring
- Checkbox to confirm administration
- Timestamp editable for backdating
- Logged medication appears in history
